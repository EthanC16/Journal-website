I am taking part in the Growth Company's 12 week software bootcamp and during the course of this I will be adding to and creating this web journal. The 12 week skills bootcamp is designed to equip learners with the skills and knowledge required to start a career in software development.

What does the course cover?

  • Week 1 - JS Fundamentals
  • Week 2 - HTML & CSS
  • Week 3 - JS DOM
  • Week 4 - Advanced Javascript
  • Week 5 - Introduction to React and Back End Development
  • Week 6 - React Next Level & CRUD Operations
  • Week 7 - Advanced React, SQL & Web Servers
  • Week 8 - Advanced React, Deployment & APIs
  • Week 9 - React Project Week and Preparing for Week 10
  • Week 10 - Final Group Projects
